The Thymeleaf logo artwork can be found at:
https://github.com/thymeleaf/thymeleaf-dist/tree/master/src/artwork/

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The are two versions of the Thymeleaf logo:

   1. With the "thymeleaf" text
   2. Without the "thymeleaf" text

The version of the logo with the "thymeleaf" text can be displayed on white,
green or dark (gray) background.

WHITE is the preferred background, and that should be considered the default
version of the logo.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Specifications:

  * Font: Ubuntu (Regular Italic) - http://font.ubuntu.com/
  * Colors:
    * White:      rgb(255,255,255) #ffffff
    * Green:      rgb(0,95,15)     #005f0f
    * Light Gray: rgb(174,169,159) #aea99f
    * Dark Gray:  rgb(51,51,51)    #333333
  * SVG creator software and format: Inkscape - https://inkscape.org/
